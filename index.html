Review on Fortnite

Fortnite is a popular battle royale game where 100 players fight to be the last one standing. Its colorful graphics and unique building mechanics make it stand out from other games in the genre. However, some may find the game's building aspect too complex, and the frequent changes to gameplay may frustrate some players. Overall, Fortnite is a fun and engaging game that can offer hours of entertainment.
